We run from shadows
And retreat to shade
But if you stop to think
Aren’t they the same thing by different names?

We trust our instincts
To tell us what we need
But if you stop to think
Not as important as we’re led to believe?

Where do, where do we go from here?
To unravel this tangled knot of fears?

Married to our vices, to our vices we are married
It’s our vices that keep us sane
Married our devices, to devices we are married
With our devices we are the same

Knot comes loose
You are the shackle, are the shackle to your chains
By now you thought you’d learn how not to be afraid
Where do we go after euphoria? Where do we go from here?
